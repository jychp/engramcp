## Responsibilities

Maintainers are expected to:

- Review and merge pull requests
- Keep the project secure and stable
- Enforce `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`
- Maintain release quality and documentation quality
- Steward architecture decisions and roadmap priorities

## Becoming a maintainer

Maintainers are appointed by existing maintainers based on sustained, high-quality contributions and constructive collaboration.

Typical signals:

- Repeated high-quality contributions (code, tests, docs, review)
- Strong understanding of project architecture and quality standards
- Reliable communication and follow-through

## Inactivity

If a maintainer becomes inactive for an extended period, active maintainers may update this file to keep ownership and response paths clear.
